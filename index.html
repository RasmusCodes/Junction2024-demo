Scootaware - electric scooters, responsibly

Our idea utilizes Huawei 3D SDK to enhance our UI and UX. Our system relies on AI to calculate and evaluate different threats. We want to make sure that no one will lose their lives in electric scooter accidents.

Prevent collisions

With machine vision, our system can create a 3D model of the environment and evaluate different hazards.

Prevent drunk driving

Upon noticing atypical and hazardous driving patterns, our AI system tries to stop the scooter safely.

User-friendly visuals

Our app would show navigation advice (ie. where to turn) very intuitively, ensuring that the driver's focus stays on the road.

3D particles enhancing user experience

Our app has 3D particles for weather, turning, and driving, making the UX more interesting.

Web demo of our solution

With this demo, you can see how difficult it is to react quickly to sudden obstacles - especially at high speeds. Luckily our automatic braking system is here to help!
